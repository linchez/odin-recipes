Meatball recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons minced onion
  • 2 table spoons water
  • 1 large egg
  • 1(8 ounce) can jerried cranberry sauce
  • 3/4 can chilli sauce
  • 1 table spoon brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice

steps of preparation

  1. Gather the ingredients.Preheat the oven to 350
  2. Mix ground beef ,bread crumbs,onions,water and egg together in a large bowl. Form into small meatballs and arrange on a nostick baking sheet.Bake in a pre heated oven for 20 to 30 minutes and turn once
  3. Stir cranberry sauce,chilli sauce, brown sugar and lemon juice together in a large sauce pan over low heat until smooth. Add meatballs;simmer for one hour before serving
