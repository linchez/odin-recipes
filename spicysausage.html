spicy sausage recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bulk spicy pork sausage
  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese or more to taste
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 2 tablespoons coconut flour
  • 2 pqackages unflavoured gelatin
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 tablespoon baking powder

steps of preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.line a jelly roll pan with parchment paper
  2. Combine sausage, ceddar cheese,almond flour,almond flour,gelatin ,egg and baking powder in a large bowl. Mix to combine using your hands,a stand mixer or a food processor.Form a mixture into uniformly-sized balls and place on the prepared pan
  3. Bake in the pre heated oven until cooked through 23 to 28 minutes. Remove sausage balls and let cool on the pan until oil is reabsorbed, about 5 minutes
