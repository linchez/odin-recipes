jalapeno recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 mini phyllo turtle shells
  • 4 ounches cream cheese,softened
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 jalapeno peppers,seeded and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon hot pepper sause
  • bacon bits

steps of preparation

  1. Preheat an oven to 360 degrees .place phyllo cups into baking sheet
  2. Stir together cream cheese,cheddar cheese, jalapenos and hot sauce in a bowl.spoon mixture into phyllo cups. sprinkle bacon bits on top.Bake in pre heated oven until golden brown about 15 to 20 minutes and serve warm.
